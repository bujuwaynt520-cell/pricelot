# PriceLot Content Strategy

# Goal

Every piece of content should help a beginner become a consistently profitable trader.

Content must always prioritize understanding over memorization.

---

# Content Philosophy

Every lesson must answer three questions:

1. What is this?
2. Why does it matter?
3. How do I use it?

Never assume prior knowledge.

---

# Lesson Structure

Every lesson should contain:

## Hero

* Title
* Short description
* Difficulty
* Estimated reading time
* Category

---

## Learning Objectives

Clear bullet points describing what the learner will understand after finishing the lesson.

---

## Lesson Body

The lesson should progress naturally.

Use:

* Definitions
* Explanations
* Practical examples
* Charts
* Diagrams
* Screenshots
* Real market examples

Avoid walls of text.

---

## Images

Every important concept should be supported by visual material whenever appropriate.

Examples:

* Candlestick diagrams
* Trend examples
* Support and resistance charts
* Market structure illustrations

---

## Videos

Lessons should include educational videos whenever they improve understanding.

Videos should complement—not replace—the written lesson.

---

## Examples

Every lesson should include multiple examples.

Examples should use realistic market situations.

---

## Common Mistakes

Explain the mistakes beginners usually make.

Also explain how to avoid them.

---

## Key Takeaways

Summarize the most important ideas.

---

## Quiz

Every lesson should include review questions.

Questions should reinforce understanding rather than memorization.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

Answer the questions beginners are most likely to ask.

---

# Writing Style

Write as though teaching one student.

Use:

* Short paragraphs
* Simple English
* Friendly tone
* Professional language

Avoid:

* Marketing language
* Fake promises
* Excessive jargon

---

# Progression

Lessons should become progressively more difficult.

Each lesson should naturally prepare the learner for the next one.

---

# Internal Linking

Every lesson should recommend:

* Previous lessons
* Related lessons
* Next lessons

Learning should feel continuous.

---

# Accuracy

Never sacrifice correctness for simplicity.

If simplifying a concept, clearly explain the limitations of the simplification.

---

# Evergreen Content

Whenever possible, write lessons so they remain useful for years.

Avoid unnecessary references to temporary market events.

---

# Ultimate Goal

A complete beginner should be able to study every PriceLot lesson in sequence and develop a professional understanding of trading.
